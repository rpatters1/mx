## Our assessment: Public Domain

No LICENSE file exists in the repository. However:

1. Every composition in this collection is by a composer who died well over 95 years ago
   (Bach, Beethoven, Chopin, Debussy, Joplin, Liszt, Mozart, Satie, Schubert, Tchaikovsky, etc.).
   The underlying works are unambiguously public domain worldwide.

2. The repository owner explicitly describes the files as "public domain" in both the
   repo description and README.

3. The files are MuseScore community score exports. MuseScore's platform encourages
   sharing of public-domain arrangements; individual score pages typically carry CC0 or
   CC-BY licenses.

4. No contrary terms (CC-BY-NC, "all rights reserved", EULA) appear anywhere in the repo.

Conclusion: treat as Public Domain. If any individual file turns out to originate from a
MuseScore page with a more restrictive license, remove it at that point.
